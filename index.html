Planning your stay

Passport

Get your passport ready! after Brexit, you cannot travel to UK using any other type of ID. Make sure your passport does not expire soon (Some companies will not allow you to travel if you have less than 6 months before expire date), if required, request a new one, depends on your country, it may take up to 1 month to renew your passport.

EHIC

It is a free card that gives you access to medically necessary, state-provided healthcare during a temporary stay in any of the 27 EU countries, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway and Switzerland or the United Kingdom under the same conditions and at the same cost (free in some countries) as for people insured in that country.

Get the EHIC here

Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA)

ETA is a required document to travel into UK, it has a cost ot £16, but it may change, you will need the passport you will be use to travel and download the application into your phone. Please complete the required steps from the app.

Get the ETA here
